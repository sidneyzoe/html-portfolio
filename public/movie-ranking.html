Sidney's Movie Recommendations

Top 3 Movie Recommendations

Spirited Away

The director is fantastic and this is an iconic movie that shows how wonderful and sweet anime can be.

Independence Day

An underrated movie that always pulls you in with its thrilling storyline, even if it's the 10th time you've seen it!

Mulan

An inspiring Disney movie with some of the best songwriting. It covers women's rights, war, and cultural values while keeping everything lighthearted with a comical dragon voiced by Eddie Murphy.
